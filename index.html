Meet Muskaan

Hi, I'm Muskaan.

Intuitive Tarot Reader  ·  Psychology Background  ·  Yoga Enthusiast

I'm an intuitive tarot reader with a background in Psychology, and I use tarot as a tool to help you understand your situations, emotions, and patterns with more clarity. My readings aren't about telling you what to do — they're about helping you see things from a deeper perspective so you can make decisions that feel right for you.

I'm also a yoga enthusiast, and I've always been drawn to practices that support emotional and spiritual healing. Before focusing on tarot, I spent several years working in animal welfare and social impact, which taught me a lot about empathy, patience, and holding space for different life journeys.
